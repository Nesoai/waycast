🧭 Why WayCast — isn't this just Google Maps?

Maps answers "how do I get there?" — turn-by-turn while you drive. WayCast answers "should I leave now, and what am I driving into?" — the forecast at five points along your route at the moment you'll be at each one, the best departure window scored by rain and traffic, sunset and glare warnings, what to wear and pack, and what the fuel run costs.
